Summer VS Winter

The Olympic Website

Summer Olympics

  • Season: Summer
  • Typical Dates: Late July to early August
  • Sports: Track & field, swimming, gymnastics, basketball, soccer, tennis, cycling, rowing
  • Frequency: Every 4 years
  • Venues: Stadiums, pools, courts, outdoor fields, velodromes
  • Average Viewers: 3-4 billion
  • Participants: Nearly all countries, averages 200; more events

Winter Olympics

  • Season: Winter
  • Typical Date: February
  • Sports: Skiing, snowboarding, ice hockey, figure skating, bobsled, curling, luge, speed skating, biathlon
  • Frequency: Every 4 years (offset 2 years from Summer)
  • Venues: Ice rinks, ski slopes, bobsled tracks, outdoor snow areas
  • Average Viewers: 2-3 billion
  • Participants: Mostly countries with winter climates, averages 90; fewer events
